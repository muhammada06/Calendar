Q1: Can I use the calendar without an internet connection?

A: Yes, you can view events offline. However, syncing and exporting require an internet connection.

Q2: How do I turn off notifications?

A: Navigate to Settings → Notifications → Toggle off notifications.

Q3: How do I back up my calendar events?

A: Go to Settings → Export Data → Choose a file format and save it.

Q4: How do I change an event’s time or date?

A: Select the event, click "Edit", modify the date/time, and save changes.

Q5: Why am I not receiving notifications for my events?

A: Check that notifications are enabled in Settings, and make sure your device allows notifications for the app.

Q6: Can I create an event that repeats automatically?

A: Yes, when adding an event, select the "Repeat" option and choose the recurrence frequency (weekly, monthly, yearly).
